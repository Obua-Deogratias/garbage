Services We Offer

We as GarbageGo provide different services to the community and these are as below;

Waste Collection Scheduling

We schedule and manage waste collection services for households, businesses, and communities. We coordinate with waste management companies or local authorities to ensure timely and efficient waste disposal.

Waste Management Reporting

We provide reporting and analytics on waste collection, recycling rates, and environmental impact to users, businesses, and local authorities.

Waste Auditing

We conduct waste audits for businesses and institutions to identify opportunities for waste reduction and recycling improvements.

ABOUT US

Garbage Go is a dedicated platform striving to make a positive impact on waste
management and environmental conservation. Our mission is to provide innovative solutions
to the growing waste management challenges faced by communities and businesses in Jinja Municipality.

We believe in promoting responsible waste disposal and recycling practices
to protect the environment. Our team is passionate about creating a cleaner and greener future for generations to come.

Our Vision

To be a leading force in transforming waste management and fostering sustainable living practices Jinja Municipality.

Our mission is to revolutionize waste management and foster a greener, more sustainable Jinja Municipality. We are dedicated to providing innovative solutions to the growing waste challenges faced by communities and businesses.
